"The quality of home, found only West of Lagos"

WOL is a collective of sisters who facilitate the import of fine textiles from West African producers for the use in traditional tailoring.

We work exclusively with small operations led and operated by women. Each fold of cloth was hand sewn and indentified in-person for quality before making its way West.

Each piece is unique, not unlike our clients. Contact us today for product availability and anything else.
